How to write a proposal

A proposal should include:
  • Executive Summary: contains a brief statement of the problem, background information. Should be short and straight to the point.
  • Statement of Need: Detail why the plan or project the proposal recommends is necessary. Present facts and evidence to support the need for the project.
  • Project Description: Explain specifics of the plan or project, and how it will go into effect and how it will be evaluated.
  • Budget Analysis: Provide and explain how the plan or project will be financed and categorize and annotate operating expenses.
  • Conclusion: Summarize the proposal’s main points. Statement that will make people interested in the project.

How to register a special project

All associations and special projects are to be registered in the third floor of the student center.

  1. Complete a registration form
  2. Describe the purpose of the team and provide a structure of the executive board. For exmaple:
    • Captain
    • Co-Captain
    • Secretary
    • Treasurer
    • Activities Coordinator
    • Workers
  3. Submit the paperwork. If approved, you know have an official project in the university!
