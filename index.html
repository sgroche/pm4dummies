Issue Statement

The University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez offers various opportunities to grow as a professional. Students from different departments are involved in research, associations and special projects. A special project can be defined as an extracurricular activity for students where they participate in a competition or presentation. There are approximately 25 special projects being developed at the university.

Our guide will be able to assist students on how to administrate a special project, starting from registration and ending with the closing process. This tool will help students improve the organization of their projects and can promote the creation of new ones.

Why do I need it?

Throughout our project we administered a questionnaire to students participating in projects of this type. The idea was to determine whether or not this was a worthwhile effort.
